Location

The wedding ceremony will take place at the Church of El Salvador, in Nerja, on Friday 19th May 2017.



The reception will be held in Hacienda Seè´–orio De Nevada. If you would like to book a room to stay the night of the wedding (19th May), please contact the hotel directly by email (reservas@senoriodenevada.es) or telephone (+34 958 777 092) quoting reference number 25162.

Information

Our wedding will take place on Friday 19th May 2017. We recommend staying a few days in Nerja before the wedding.

The nearest airport to Nerja is Malaga. EasyJet, Aer Lingus and Ryanair all fly from Belfast to Malaga daily. There are lots of car rental options at Malaga airport. It is also possible to travel by bus from the airport to Nerja.

There are loads of nice places to stay in Nerja - have a look at Booking.com or Owners Direct.


On the day of the wedding there will be a bus provided which will take you from the church to the wedding reception, returning to Nerja later in the evening.

For those staying the night at Hacienda Seè´–orio de Nevada you may wish to drive so you have your car the following day. Please indicate whether or not you intend to drive or avail of the bus when submitting your RSVP below.
